2017-2018 Game Schedule

USC Badminton Club plays in the Southern California Collegiate Badminton League (SCCBL). Every year we travel across Southern California to compete against regional schools for top team in the league. Additonally, we participate in USA Badminton Collegiate Nationals when possible. In 2016 we placed 1st in Collegiate Nationals and placed 3rd in 2017!
