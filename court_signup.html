What is my USC ID number?

Your USC ID number is your USC student ID

What is my USC NetID?

Your USC NetID is your USC email username. If your email address is ttrojan@usc.edu, your USC NetID is ttrojan

Court Rules
  1. Court reservation is only open during weekly practice times.
  2. If there is currently no one on the court you signed up for, you will immediately be placed on court until the limit of 4 players per court is reached.
  3. If there are players on the court you signed up, you will be placed on the court's waitlist and a 15-minute timer will start for the players on court.
